About Me

Hello! My name is Johnson Amodu. I am a Certified Data Scientist from Explore Data Science Academy. I have 4+ years experience as a Data Analyst and 2+ experience as a Full Stack Data Scientist with specialization in Business Intelligence Analytics and Machine Learning.

I am passionate about helping businesses unlock the full potentials in their large datasets. I also help other professionals, particularly those with non-technical backgrounds, explore the possibilities of switching to data science or other data analytics fields. Through my experiences, I hope to provide valuable insights and guidance for those considering a similar career transition.

I work with data and other technical professionals to make data driven decisions and to build applications using Python, SQL, Excel, PowerBI, Tableau, Machine Learning frameworks, Cloud tools(AWS, Azure and Heroku), SPSS, SASS and basically any tool suitable for the task at hand.

I am a graduate of Petroleum Engineering(M.Sc & B.Sc) from the University of Ibadan. At my final year, I was privileged to serve as the Chapter President of the Society of Petroleum Engineers, University of Ibadan, Ibadan.

I have a background in teaching high school Mathematics and Physics, but made a career switch to become a data scientist specializing in business intelligence analytics and model deployment.

I love music and I attend musical concerts at my leisure time. I play the bass guitar and the piano as well. Don't get it twisted when you see me perform in live band.

I left a 10-year teaching career to become a data scientist. Now, I help others learn and grow in this field.

It's never too late to pursue your passion!
